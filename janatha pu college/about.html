About us

Janatha Independent PU.College provides flexible access for the students to pursue their passion and translate their ideas into actions by supporting every individual under its care. A vibrant campus and congenial atmosphere enable our students to develop skills and build talents that last a lifetime. We also offer excellent education with support of talented and highly proficient teaching and supporting staff who are committed to the cause of quality education.

We proudly commit ourselves to prepare every young student to develop a passion to serve the society by the way of faith, hope and love. At Janatha Independent PU.College , every student is trained to make a positive contribution to the communities in which they live and work..
